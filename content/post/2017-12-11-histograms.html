library(sqldf)
library(Lahman)
library(ggplot2)

Histograms are data visualizations that use one value of data.

query<-"SELECT weight
FROM Master"

result<-sqldf(query)

ggplot()+
  geom_histogram(data=result,aes(x=weight),color="green",fill="white",bins=50)+
  ggtitle("Body Weight Distribution for Baseball Players")
